## Symptoms
- See a 403 or 404 error when opening docs
- Links do not load

## Quick Fix
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Login Status">
    Ensure you are logged in with your Дмитрий Белоусов account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browser Refresh">
    Try refreshing the page or clearing browser cache.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permissions">
    Confirm your account has access to this documentation space.
  </Step>
</Steps>
