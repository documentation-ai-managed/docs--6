## Overview

You can create categories to organize your projects under Дмитрий Белоусов’s documentation.

## Steps

1. Click "Add New Project" on your dashboard.
2. Enter a project name and description.
3. Assign it to a category (e.g., Personal, Work).
4. Save changes.

## Tip

Use descriptive project names for easy searching.
