description: Quick guide to adding a project in Дмитрий Белоусов.
---

## Overview

Create new project documentation summaries with ease.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Projects">
    Go to your Дмитрий Белоусов dashboard and click "Projects".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Add New">
    Select "Add New Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in Details">
    Enter the project name, summary, and assign to a category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click "Save" to complete the process.
  </Step>
</Steps>
