## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: Project files ready to upload (Markdown, images, or code files)

<Callout kind="info">
  No credit card required for the free tier. Upgrade later for advanced features.
</Callout>

## Sign Up and Create Account

Follow these steps to register your account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.example.com/signup`.

    Click the **Sign Up** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and accept the terms.

    Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Select your role (Developer, Manager, or Admin) and set your brand color (default: `#3B82F6`).

    You now land on the main dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

Organize projects by creating dedicated spaces.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Space" icon="settings">
    Enter a name like "My Project Docs", add a description, and choose visibility (Public or Private).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and Enter" icon="arrow-right">
    Save the space. It appears in your sidebar. Click to enter.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Upload Initial Project Files

Add your existing files to the space.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Drag & Drop" icon="upload">
    Open your space and drag files directly into the file browser.

    Supported formats: `.md`, `.mdx`, `.png`, `.jpg`, `.js`.

    Files upload automatically with progress indicators.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="File Picker" icon="folder">
    Click **Upload Files** in the toolbar.

    Select multiple files from your computer and confirm.

    Organize into folders post-upload.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Limit initial uploads to `<10MB` per file for optimal performance.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Document

Build a simple page using MDX.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    In your space, click **New Document**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="type">
    Replace default text with your content. Use the toolbar for headings and lists.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="globe">
    Toggle preview, then click **Publish** to make it live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Here is a starter MDX example:

<CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Markdown">
  ```mdx
  ---
  title: My First Doc
  description: Welcome to my project.
  ---

  ## Hello World

  This is your first **MDX** document.
  ```
  ```markdown
  # My First Doc (Markdown)

  ## Hello World

  This is plain Markdown.
  ```
</CodeGroup>
